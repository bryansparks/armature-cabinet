# Headline rubric

A blog headline carries the message's claim — it doesn't inflate it.

- **Clear over clever.** The reader should know what the post is about before
  they click. A pun is fine; a mystery-box is not.
- **The claim front-loaded.** The message's sharpest point, in plain words.
- **No clickbait.** Don't promise something the post doesn't deliver. "You won't
  believe…" is a flag, not a headline.
- **Faithful.** The headline reflects the message; it doesn't manufacture a
  stronger angle the message doesn't have.

Three candidates minimum — give the user options. Flag any that over-claim.

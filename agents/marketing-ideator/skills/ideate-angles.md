cost_tier: T2
outputs: Angles
---

1. Take the seed (a frustration, a question, a product truth) as input.
2. Generate 3+ distinct angles per the rubric — each a way to frame the seed for
   a specific audience (the frustrated, the curious, the skeptical).
3. Each angle must be faithful — from the seed, not invented. No fabricated
   benefits.
4. Return `Angles` (each: the angle line, the audience it targets, why it
   resonates, confidence, the seed it rests on).
5. Flag any angle that over-claims or misrepresents the brand.

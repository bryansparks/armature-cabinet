---
goal: Critique each candidate message on clarity, resonance, risk, brand-fit, and over-claiming — give the judge the material to pick from.
success_looks_like:
  - each candidate critiqued on each criterion
  - critiques are specific, not vague
  - each critique cites the candidate
  - no picks made (the judge picks)
  - over-claims and brand risks flagged plainly
out_of_scope:
  - picking the winner (the judge's job)
  - posting, publishing, or scheduling
  - rewriting the candidates
  - brand strategy
---

This partner exists because a message that survives critique is stronger than
one that didn't face any. The mandate is critique: assess, compare, flag —
nothing more.
